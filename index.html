Formative Films, Flimsy Representation

Examining the representation of women in movies from my formative years

I have always loved movies and can most certainly say that the majority of my personality and my understanding of my sense of self are the very least non-reductive, stories about women, people of color, and basically anyone who is not a cis-hetero white man. I wanted to deeply derived from the things I grew up watching. Unfortunately, Hollywood has historically done a bad job at telling nuanced, or at examine the stories that influenced (or had the potential to influence) me when I was growing up.

What is the Bechdel test?

A way of evaluating whether or not a film or other work of fiction portrays women in a way that is sexist or characterized by gender ste- reotyping.

To pass the Bechdel test a work must feature:

  • At least two women
  • These women must talk to each other, and
  • Their conversation must concern something other than a man.
